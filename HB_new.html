Our Specialized Laboratories

The RS Lab at IIT Kanpur houses five distinct laboratory units, each dedicated to a
specialized domain of Earth surface and river science.

UAV Data Processing

Dedicated to high-resolution photogrammetry and point-cloud analysis. We process LiDAR, thermal, and hyperspectral data captured by our drone fleet.

Sedimentology Lab

Physical and Equipped for physical and chemical characterization of riverine and floodplain sediments. Focuses on grain-size distributions and mineralogy.

Remote Sensing & GIS Lab

The central hub for satellite Earth Observation data. We develop spatial models for basin-wide health monitoring.

Core Archive Lab

Houses extensive collections of sediment drill cores from the Himalayan foreland for Quaternary climate reconstruction.

CZO Lab

Critical Zone Observatory Lab focuses on the complex interactions between soil, water, and air in agriculture-dominated Ganga plains.

Ongoing Research

Pushing the Frontiers of
Spatial Science

Our research projects integrate multi-scale remote sensing data with rigorous field validation to solve critical hydrological and geomorphic challenges.

Geomorphology

Earth Surface Dynamics

A core focus is Critical Zone Science (CZO), where researchers utilize drone based geomorphic mapping and landscape evolution modeling across various spatio temporal scales to understand surface dynamics.

Status: Ongoing
Sedimentology

Stratigraphy & Paleoclimate

Research efforts are centered on subsurface stratigraphic mapping using geophysical surveys, integrated with fluvial stratigraphy to build a comprehensive picture of basin evolution.

Status: Ongoing
Hydro-geology

Groundwater Dynamics

Advanced hydrogeological investigations integrating time series analysis to identify long-term trends and climate-induced patterns in aquifer storage.

Status: Ongoing
Hydrology

River Science

Focusing on river dynamics to understand channel evolution through precise sediment measurement and sophisticated hydrological modelling.

Status: Ongoing
Hazards

Risk Assessment

Quantifying flood risks through hazard mapping and monitoring water quality parameters to detect pollutants and prevent ecological degradation.
